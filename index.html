Research

The primary aim of my research is to democratize the capabilities of Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI) by extending its reach to encompass a multitude of Indic languages. With the advent of cutting-edge technologies like Generative AI, the potential for creative content generation, language translation, and cultural expression has surged remarkably. However, there exists a notable gap in its application to Indic languages, which are rich in diversity and heritage. By bridging this gap, my research endeavors to empower communities that communicate through Indic languages to leverage Generative AI for content creation, communication, and artistic expression. This pursuit holds the promise of not only preserving and revitalizing linguistic traditions but also fostering innovation and inclusivity in the digital era.

Projects

LLAMA2-finetuned model on Odia domain knowledge dataset

Introducing the LLAMA2-finetuned model, a cutting-edge linguistic AI developed through refinement with an Odia domain knowledge dataset. This model showcases remarkable language understanding and generation capabilities tailored specifically for the Odia language, offering a powerful tool for diverse applications within this linguistic domain.

Dementia dataset creation and analysis in Malayalam

Delving into the realm of cognitive health, this endeavor involves the meticulous creation and subsequent analysis of a dementia dataset in the Malayalam language. Through this initiative, a comprehensive understanding of dementia's linguistic manifestations and potential insights within the Malayalam-speaking population is poised to emerge.

Segmentation of Malayalam Palm leaf images using FCN

Embarking on the convergence of technology and heritage, this project focuses on the application of Fully Convolutional Networks (FCN) for the intricate task of segmenting Malayalam palm leaf images. By leveraging advanced deep learning techniques, this undertaking seeks to unlock new avenues for preserving and interpreting the rich cultural and historical significance encapsulated within these unique artifacts.

Olive: An Instruction following Odia GPT Model

Introducing "Olive," an innovative Odia language model built upon the GPT architecture, distinguished by its unique emphasis on instruction-based comprehension. Designed to adeptly follow and respond to instructions, Olive represents a remarkable stride in natural language processing, poised to facilitate seamless human-AI interactions and enhance various applications within the Odia linguistic landscape.

Bangalore Pollution data analysis using Kalman filter

Embarking on an investigative journey, this study delves into the analysis of Bangalore's pollution data from 2018 to 2021, employing the robust Kalman filter methodology. Through this approach, a comprehensive understanding of the city's air quality emerges, encompassing major pollutants such as particulate matter (PM2.5 and PM10), nitrogen dioxide (NO2), sulfur dioxide (SO2), carbon monoxide (CO), and ozone (O3). This multifaceted analysis promises to shed light on pollution trends, fluctuations, and potential insights for sustainable urban development.

IPL Auction System

Crafted upon the foundations of Java, the IPL Auction System stands as a testament to technological innovation in the realm of cricket. This dynamic system orchestrates the intricate processes of player bidding, team strategies, and financial management, encapsulating the essence of the Indian Premier League's high-stakes player acquisition. With Java's versatility at its core, the IPL Auction System elegantly manages the complexities of team dynamics, player valuations, and real-time updates, underscoring the fusion of sports and technology in contemporary sporting landscapes.
